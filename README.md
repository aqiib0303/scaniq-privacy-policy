## Overview

ScanIQ is a QR code and barcode scanner and generator app. 
We are committed to protecting your privacy. 
This policy explains clearly what data we collect, 
how we use it, and your rights over it.

---

## Data We Collect

### Data Stored Only On Your Device
The following data is created by you and stored 
**only on your device**. We never see it, 
access it, or upload it to any server:

- **Scan history** — barcodes and QR codes you have scanned
- **Generated QR codes** — QR codes you have created
- **App settings** — your theme, sound, and haptic preferences

You can delete all of this at any time from 
Settings → Clear Scan History or Clear Product Cache.

### Data Sent to Third Parties

When you scan a **product barcode** (EAN-13, UPC, etc.) 
and tap "Lookup Product", the barcode number is sent to:

**Open Food Facts** (https://world.openfoodfacts.org)

This is a free, open-source food product database.
- Only the barcode number is sent — nothing else
- No personal information is attached to this request
- Open Food Facts privacy policy: 
  https://world.openfoodfacts.org/privacy

---

## Permissions We Request

| Permission | Why We Need It |
|---|---|
| **Camera** | To scan QR codes and barcodes |
| **Internet** | To look up product information from Open Food Facts |
| **Vibrate** | To give haptic feedback when a scan is detected |
| **Storage (Android 9 and below)** | To save generated QR code images to your gallery |
| **Read Media Images (Android 13+)** | To save generated QR code images to your gallery |

We do not use any permission for tracking, 
advertising, or data collection.

---

## Data We Do NOT Collect

We want to be fully transparent. ScanIQ does **not** collect:

- Your name, email, or any account information
- Your location
- Your device ID or advertising ID
- Any analytics or crash reports
- Any usage statistics
- Any data for advertising purposes

---

## Third Party Services

ScanIQ uses the following third party services:

### Open Food Facts
- **Purpose:** Product barcode lookup
- **What is sent:** Barcode number only
- **Their privacy policy:** https://world.openfoodfacts.org/privacy

No other third party SDKs, analytics tools, 
or advertising networks are used in this app.

---

## Data Security

All data stored by ScanIQ lives in your device's 
private app storage. It is not included in 
cloud backups (Google Drive backup). 
It is only transferred to a new device 
if you use Android's device-to-device transfer feature.

---

## Children's Privacy

ScanIQ does not knowingly collect any data from 
children under the age of 13. The app contains 
no features that target children specifically.

## Your Rights

Since we store no personal data on our servers, 
there is nothing for us to delete or export on request. 
All your data is on your own device and fully 
under your control. You can delete everything 
by uninstalling the app.
